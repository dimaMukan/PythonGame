
            Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Welcome to the game!
Game was made by Oleh Kovalets and Dima Mukan,
students of KNU University IPZ-22 group

Rules:
You should choose one thing of the following:
rock, paper, scissors, lizard or spock.

        Scissors cuts Paper
        Paper covers Rock
        Rock crushes Lizard
        Lizard poisons Spock
        Spock smashes Scissors
        Scissors decapitates Lizard
        Lizard eats Paper
        Paper disproves Spock
        Spock vaporizes Rock
        Rock crushes Scissors
